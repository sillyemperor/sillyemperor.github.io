经纬度转WebMercator坐标

Published: Tue 04 June 2019
By Wang Jiang

In GIS.

tags: GIS WebMercator 经纬度

输入x_lon,y_lat

输出x_mercator，y_mercator

num = x_lon * 0.017453292519943295
x = 6378137.0 * num
a = y_lat * 0.017453292519943295
x_mercator = x
y_mercator = 3189068.5 * math.log((1.0 + math.sin(a)) / (1.0 - math.sin(a)))
